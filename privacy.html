PRIVACY POLICY

EFFECTIVE: JUNE 27, 2026 · SITE: STARSHIP-TRACKER.SPACE

This Privacy Policy describes how starship-tracker.space ("we", "our", or "the site") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. Third-party services we use may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site — which pages are most popular and how users navigate.

Google Analytics uses cookies to track anonymous usage data. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

3. Google AdSense

We display advertisements provided by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history.

  • You can opt out of personalized ads at Google Ads Settings.
  • See Google's Advertising & Privacy FAQ for more details.

4. Cookies

Our site itself does not set cookies directly. Google Analytics and Google AdSense set cookies on your device. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party APIs

Launch data is fetched from Launch Library 2 and the SpaceX Data API. These are read-only public APIs — no personal data is sent to them when you visit this site.

6. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
